WEB UI ANALYTICS SURVEY

This is a survey over UI design color preferences where you will go through a series of web pages which consist of 3 genres of websites, each having 3 variants in color.
The genres of websites in the order of appearance would be:

  • Social Networking
  • Online Shopping
  • Medical Services
At the end of the series of pages, there will be a form where you need to enter your details and give your feedback.
Note: The pages that you go through are dummy pages and are just for viewing purposes.

Instructions

  • Every Page would have a next button to the right of the page. Click to go to the next page.
  • Every Page would require a rating from you at the end. So, keep a rating in mind for every page as you are previewing them.
